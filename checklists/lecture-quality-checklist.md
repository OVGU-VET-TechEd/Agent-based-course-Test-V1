# Lecture Quality Checklist

## 1. Outline & Agenda
- [ ] Are all Learning Objectives from `lecture-outline.md` covered by at least one session in `lecture-agenda.md`?
- [ ] Is the total time of all sessions in the agenda consistent with the `time-commitment` in the outline?

## 2. Didactics & Persona
- [ ] Is the `professor-persona` from `lecture-didactics.md` clear and well-defined?
- [ ] Is the `style` (e.g., humorous, academic) from `lecture-didactics.md` reflected in the `materials/` files?

## 3. Materials
- [ ] Does every session in the agenda have a corresponding `materials/` file?
- [ ] Are all `materials/` files written in valid LiaScript/Markdown format?
- [ ] Are code examples correct and well-commented?
- [ ] Are all references and sources cited?
